34      /**
35       * Return all options for paying the mana cost (this) while taking into accoutn if the player can pay life.
36       * Used to correctly highlight (or not) spells with Phyrexian mana depending on if the player can pay life costs.
37       * <p>
38       * E.g. Tezzeret's Gambit has a cost of {3}{U/P}.
39       * If `canPayLifeCost == true` the two options are {3}{U} and {3}. The second including a 2 life cost that is not
40       * captured by the ManaOptions object being returned.
41       * However, if `canPayLifeCost == false` than the return is only {3}{U} since the Phyrexian mana MUST be paid with
42       * a {U} and not with 2 life.
43       *
44       * @param canPayLifeCost if the player is able to pay life for the ability/effect that this cost is associated with
45       * @return
46       */
